Welcome to Troop 370

Boy Scout Troop 370 is proud to have completed over 40 years of consecutive service to the Buckhead\Brookhaven community. Sponsored by St. James United Methodist Church, Troop 370 has a long history of developing boys into young men following the aims and ideals of the Boy Scouts of America movement.
